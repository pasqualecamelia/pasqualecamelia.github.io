My research is organized into three main lines: (1) projection / finite-rank / time, (2) boundary / wave / observability, and (3) Giza / metrology / construction.

Across these lines, my work develops a structural approach in which observability, metric structure, ordering, and constructional form are treated as consequences of projection, finite admissibility, and relational invariance, rather than as primitive givens.
